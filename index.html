About Us

AMWAJ AL SAFOOH

Technical Services LLC

Established to make a difference, Amwaj Company is your trusted partner in delivering specialized engineering and manpower services. Our journey began in 2023, and our central hub is situated in the vibrant city of Hor Al Anz, UAE. We are renowned for our commitment to excellence and our diverse portfolio of trade and service offerings across the United Arab Emirates and beyond.

Company Services

We Provide Most Exclusive
Service For Engineering

Mechanical

We take pride in offering a comprehensive suite of Mechanical Services designed to ensure the efficient functioning of HVAC, Refrigeration , Piping and other mechanical systems in residential, commercial, and industrial spaces.

Electrical

We offer a wide range of electrical services, including Air Conditioning System(AC) ,LV, MV and ELV System, Power Distribution System, Lighting and Generator services, and more. Our solutions prioritize safety, reliability, and energy efficiency.

Plumbing

We are your trusted source for comprehensive Plumbing Services designed to ensure efficient water distribution and drainage systems for residential, commercial, and industrial spaces.

Fire Fighting

We offer comprehensive MEP Fire Fighting services for safety and protection, including Fire Alarm, Sprinkler, Extinguisher Inspection, Hydrant, and Suppression Systems in various spaces.

Civil

Our experienced team offers crucial Designing and Drafting services for MEP solutions, creating precise technical plans for mechanical, electrical, and plumbing systems in diverse spaces.
